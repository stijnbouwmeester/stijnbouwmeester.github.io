About the site

This website's purpose is to be a personal blog, and a gallery of various projects of mine.
It is written in HTML and CSS, and hosted on Neocities!
Tools used: Sublime Text 4, Aseprite, Adobe Fireworks

This site has been up on one form or another since 2013.
It was initially inspired by 2008 personal sites that girls used to make for handmade pixel graphics, and Japanese website material sites.
My goals for this site would be to fill it up with lots of happy memories, and to display a collection of things I like that I want other people to know about.
